About Us

The Disaster Relief Management is dedicated to the mission of saving lives by providing relief and information management solutions that enable organizations and communities to better respond to disasters aftermath. We develop free and open source software and provide services that help solve concrete problems and bring efficiencies to disaster response coordination between aid organizations, civil society and the victims themselves.
